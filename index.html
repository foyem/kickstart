Apply Now!

Overview & Criteria

The MyJobMag Career Kickstart Scholarship is designed to support job seekers and career changers across the country who are looking to enhance their skills, obtain certifications, or pursue training programs that align with their career goals. This scholarship aims to help individuals overcome financial barriers that might prevent them from acquiring the necessary skills or qualifications for their desired job roles. The scholarship grant could be used for online courses, certifications, workshops, or professional development programs.

  • Citizenship: Nigerian citizen or legal resident.
  • Age: 18+.
  • Employment Status: Unemployed, underemployed, or pursuing a career change.
  • Intent: Clear plan for utilizing the grant to advance career prospects.
  • Financial Need: Preference for applicants with demonstrated financial need.
  • Application Requirement: A 500-word essay or video on career goals, current barriers, and how the scholarship will help.
