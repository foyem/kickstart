MYJOBMAG Career Kickstart Scholarship 2025

Empowering Your Next Career Move: Unlock Skills and Opportunities with the MyJobMag Career Kickstart Scholarship

Apply Now!

Overview & Criteria

Are you ready to take your career to the next level but facing financial hurdles? MyJobMag, Nigeria's leading talent development and recruitment company, is excited to bring you the MyJobMag Career Kickstart Scholarship 2024. This scholarship is designed to provide aspiring job seekers and career changers across Nigeria with the opportunity to gain new skills, obtain valuable certifications, and participate in training programs that align with their career goals. Whether you're looking to advance in your current field or switch to a new one, this scholarship aims to help you achieve your goal without the financial burden.

  • Citizenship: Nigerian citizen or legal resident.
  • Age: 18+.
  • Employment Status: Unemployed, underemployed, or pursuing a career change.
Apply Now !

How To Apply

Ready to kickstart your career journey?
Follow these steps:
1. Fill out the online application form on this website.
2. Submit a 500-word essay or video sharing your career goals, the financial challenges you're facing, and how this scholarship will help you achieve your ambitions.
The application window will run from 5th November 2024 to 5th of December 2024. Shortlisted applicants will be contacted shortly after the application deadline, and successful candidates will begin their training in January 2025.

  • Application OpensNovember 1, 2024
  • Application Deadline December 2, 2024
  • Review Period December 2 - December 20, 2024.
  • Winners Announced January 15, 2025.
